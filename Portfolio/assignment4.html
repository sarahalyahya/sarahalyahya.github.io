The Sad Life of Kovid

An Interactive Film
link documentation

Sometimes I look back and cannot believe that this is actually a project we made. It started from wanting to create a mockumentary, to wanting to create a parody of reality tv, to slowly choosing Kovid with a K as our protagonist. I remember questioning myself and asking whether or not this was our ACTUAL idea. Turns out, it was!

I think this assignment presented me with a different challenge. We had tried to recruit another person to play our protagonist, however, I ended up having to do it because our friends from outside the course weren’t too thrilled about the role (can you imagine?!) I haven’t acted in years, and the thought of having my obnoxious character perceived by my groupmates editing and the class made me feel a bit insecure, but nonetheless I dedicated myself to our ridiculous idea and I’m so glad I pushed myself to do this role!

Other than working on the script and playing Kovid, I also did some visuals for this assignment, particularly the “Kovidgram” brand and color scheme.

We had a blast filming and working on this in general! The shoots were like a moment of ease in a very chaotic time in the semester, and the editing process led to some fascinating conversations on our group chat. We got along amazingly well and we were all on the same page — suggesting similar ideas at the same time.
