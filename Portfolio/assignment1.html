Memory Lane

A film made in 30 minutes
link documentation

For this project, we filmed and edited 30 minute films in groups, and then were asked to create a website for it individually. My main inspiration for the website was blockbuster movie websites as they stick to a theme, are simple, and straight to the point. I followed a similar approach with minimal, unified interactions and stuck to an earth-tone based color palette.

My biggest challenge was creating a website that is responsive and proportional. Looking back at the code, I see that I went back and forth a lot between several positioning units. It looked fine, but the behind the scenes were just a bit messy. I also faced a challenge of being scared to create my very first website. Everything felt like it looked out of place, and then it all came together nicely!

The first instance of my interest in scribbles and collages appeared in this project. I decided to scribble draw my partner and I, create a doodley logo, and doodle over some of the website visuals. This DIY-esque approach persisted throughout the semester and manifested itself in different ways each project.
